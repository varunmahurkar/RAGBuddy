# 🤖 RAGBuddy

### *The "Buddy" RAG that works harder so you don't have to.*

**RAGBuddy** is a high-speed, easy-to-use simple Retrieval-Augmented Generation system that functions as a personal wikipedia for your documents. It is designed for effectiveness, efficiency, simplicity and performance, it removes the complexity of traditional RAG by eliminating the need for heavy, expensive pre-computation.

Instead of force-indexing every detail upfront, RAGBuddy uses **Just-in-Time Knowledge Mapping**. It generates lightweight entity relationships and summaries at the moment of the query, ensuring your AI always works with the most relevant context without the high compute "tax."

---

## 💡 Key Philosophy

*   **Instant Utility:** Like a wiki, it’s designed to be browsed and queried immediately, skipping the "indexing wall" that slows down other systems.
*   **On-Demand Intelligence:** It builds knowledge structures dynamically, focusing only on the information needed to answer the specific question at hand.
*   **Resource Efficient:** By avoiding massive background processing, it keeps your operational costs low while maintaining high-precision answers.

---

**RAGBuddy** turns your static data into an active, searchable, and intelligent buddy—no heavy lifting required.
